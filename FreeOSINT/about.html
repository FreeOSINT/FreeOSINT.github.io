About FreeOSINT.org

FreeOSINT.org is dedicated to providing high-quality, accessible education in Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) techniques and methodologies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize OSINT knowledge and empower individuals and organizations with the skills to ethically gather, analyze, and utilize publicly available information. We believe that OSINT, when practiced responsibly, can be a powerful tool for research, security, and informed decision-making.

What We Offer

  • Free, comprehensive training modules on various OSINT techniques
  • Interactive learning experiences with practical exercises
  • Ethical guidelines for responsible OSINT practice
  • Community resources for continued learning and development

Our Approach

We take a hands-on, practical approach to OSINT education. Our training modules are designed to be accessible to beginners while providing valuable insights for experienced practitioners. We emphasize ethical considerations throughout our curriculum, ensuring that learners understand not just how to use OSINT techniques, but when and why they should be used.

Join Our Community

Whether you're a security professional, researcher, journalist, or simply curious about OSINT, we welcome you to our community. Start exploring our training modules today and enhance your OSINT capabilities.
